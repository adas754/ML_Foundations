Comparison analysis -> it involves comparing different variables,groups, or scenario to identify similarities, differences, pattern and trends.
Eg- compare sales and profit across all the regions.

contribution analysis -> contribution analysis is powerful tool that helps business and organizations to analyze the contribution of various factotors towards 
particular outcome.

eg- analyze the contribution of categories towards sales, revenue.


pareto analysis -> pareto analysis also known as 80/20 rule, is a management tool used to identify the most significant factors or causes that contribute to particular 

outcome .in pareto analysis it is assumed that 80% of the effect are caused by 20% of the factor.
